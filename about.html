Our Journey

From a small dream to industry leader in heating solutions

Founded in 1998

Started with a vision to revolutionize industrial heating. Mr. Rajesh Aggarwal founded Aggarwal Heaters with 5 engineers in a 500 sq.ft facility.

Initial focus on Band Heaters for plastic industry. Quick recognition for quality and reliability.

"One thing perfectly, rather than many things averagely" - This philosophy became our foundation.

Our Evolution

1998

Company Founded

Started with 5 engineers in 500 sq.ft facility. Focused on Band Heater manufacturing.

2003

First Expansion

Expanded to 2000 sq.ft facility. Introduced Cartridge Heaters. Reached 50 customers.

2008

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015. 12 product types. Team grew to 20+ employees. 200+ customers.

2013

National Recognition

All India presence. 18 product types. Bangalore office. 300+ customers.

2018

Global Expansion

Exporting to SE Asia, Middle East, Africa. 24 products launched. 35+ team members.

2023

Industry Leader

500+ customers. 50+ team members. 5000 sq.ft facility. Industry recognition.
