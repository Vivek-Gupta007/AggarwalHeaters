Mica Strip Heaters

Mica Strip Heaters are compact and cost-effective heating elements designed for flat surface heating applications. Manufactured using high-quality nickel-chrome resistance wire insulated with premium mica sheets, these heaters provide uniform heat transfer, reliable electrical insulation, and excellent performance at moderate operating temperatures.

Uniform heat distribution across flat surfaces
High dielectric strength mica insulation
Energy-efficient and economical heating solution
Simple installation with durable construction
