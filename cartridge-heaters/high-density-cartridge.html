High-Density Cartridge Heaters

High-Density Cartridge Heaters are precision-engineered heating elements designed for high-temperature and high-watt density applications. Constructed using premium nickel-chrome resistance wire and compacted magnesium oxide insulation, these heaters deliver rapid heat-up, uniform temperature distribution, and superior thermal efficiency.

High watt density for rapid heating
Uniform heat distribution across entire length
Compact cylindrical design for tight installations
Long operational life under demanding conditions

Key Features

High Watt Density Performance

Designed to operate at high watt densities, ensuring fast heat-up time and precise temperature control even in high-load industrial applications.

Superior Thermal Conductivity

Magnesium oxide insulation provides excellent heat transfer from the resistance coil to the sheath, minimizing heat loss and maximizing efficiency.

Durable Sheath Construction

Available in stainless steel and Incoloy sheathing for corrosion resistance and high-temperature durability.

Precision Fit & Customization

Manufactured with tight tolerances for perfect bore fit, ensuring efficient heat transfer and longer service life.

Ideal Applications

Plastic Injection Molds

Used for core and cavity heating to maintain uniform melt temperature and reduce cycle time.

Die Casting

Provides localized heating in dies for smooth metal flow and improved casting quality.

Hot Runner Systems

Ensures accurate temperature control for plastic processing systems.
