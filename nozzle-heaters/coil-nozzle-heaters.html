Coil Nozzle Heaters

Coil Nozzle Heaters

Coil Nozzle Heaters are high-performance heating elements specifically designed for plastic injection molding nozzles and hot runner systems. Engineered with nickel-chrome resistance wire and high-grade insulation, these heaters provide fast heat-up, precise temperature control, and uniform heat distribution even in compact spaces.

Fast heat-up and rapid thermal response
Uniform heating across nozzle surface
Flexible design for compact installations
High durability under continuous operation

Key Features

High Watt Density Design

Flexible coil structure allows perfect fit on complex nozzle geometries, ensuring uniform heat transfer and reduced heat loss.
