Mica Nozzle Heaters

Mica Nozzle Heaters are compact and efficient heating solutions specially designed for plastic injection molding machine nozzles. Built using high-grade nickel-chrome resistance wire and premium mica insulation, these heaters provide uniform heat transfer, excellent electrical insulation, and reliable performance at moderate temperatures.

Uniform and controlled nozzle heating
High-quality mica insulation for safety
Energy-efficient and cost-effective design
