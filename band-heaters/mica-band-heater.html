Mica Band Heaters

Mica Band Heaters are a specialized type of band heater constructed using high-quality nickel-chrome resistance wire insulated with premium-grade mica. They are designed specifically for heating machine barrels, nozzles, and cylindrical surfaces with fast heat-up time and efficient heat transfer.

Fast heat transfer with mica insulation
Slim design for space-saving installation
Suitable for medium temperature applications

Key Features

Efficient Heat Transfer

Mica insulation provides excellent thermal conductivity, ensuring quick and uniform heat distribution across the barrel surface. This results in reduced heat-up time and improved machine productivity.

Compact & Lightweight Design

The slim profile allows installation in tight spaces where ceramic band heaters may not fit. Lightweight construction reduces mechanical stress on the barrel.

Cost-Effective Solution

Mica band heaters offer an economical heating option for medium temperature applications without compromising performance and reliability.

Easy Installation & Replacement

Designed with user-friendly clamping systems and terminal options, allowing quick installation and minimal downtime during maintenance.

Ideal Applications

Injection Molding

Used for heating plastic processing barrels to maintain consistent melt temperature. Ensures smooth polymer flow and improved product quality.

Extrusion

Provides uniform barrel heating in extrusion lines, maintaining consistent material viscosity for stable production output.

Packaging & Sealing Machinery

Suitable for heating components in packaging equipment where controlled medium temperature heating is required.
