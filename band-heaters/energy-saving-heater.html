Key Features

Reduced Energy Consumption

Integrated high-density insulation significantly reduces heat loss to the environment, lowering overall electricity usage by up to 30% compared to conventional heaters.

Improved Thermal Efficiency

Enhanced heat retention ensures that more heat is transferred directly to the barrel or die, improving process stability and reducing heating cycles.

Lower Surface Temperature

Outer insulation jacket reduces external surface temperature, improving workplace safety and reducing ambient heat buildup.

Long Service Life

Reduced thermal stress and controlled heating cycles extend heater lifespan while maintaining consistent performance.

Ideal Applications

Injection Molding

Helps reduce power consumption in plastic injection molding machines while maintaining stable barrel temperatures.

Extrusion

Improves energy efficiency in continuous extrusion processes by minimizing heat loss from long barrel sections.

High-Energy Consumption Plants

Ideal for manufacturing units aiming to reduce operational costs and meet energy efficiency standards.
